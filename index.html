HackWeekend

Whether you want to learn how to code, hack on a project, or get inspired, we have something for you. HackWeekend is a two day workshop and competition for students to learn from the best in tech and build awesome projects.

May 31 - June 1
Facebook HQ in Menlo Park
Talks, Demos, and Prizes
All High School and College Students Invited
Register Now

Amazing Speakers

From the Chief Technology Officer at Facebook to Tesla Founder Elon Musk to a former astronaut from NASA, we've brought together the most interesting people we could find to talk to us about their ideas and give us advice.

Interactive Workshops

Begin or level up your tech skills through our workshops with the sponsors. Learn how to code, solder an Arduino, or do a hands on exhibit on how Pebble was built with blah.

Hackathon Competition

Compete with the best student developers in the Bay Area for many great prizes provided for our sponsors. You can hack all day to build your project, or showcase in our low key exhibition hall on Sunday.
